# Compliance matrix & requirement categories

## Matrix format

| # | Requirement (from spec) | Spec clause / standard | Subject value / evidence | Verdict | Note / action |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | e.g. Min. compressive strength 7 N/mm² | Sec 04 20 00, cl. 2.3 / BS EN 771 | Datasheet: 7.5 N/mm² | Complies | — |
| 2 | Fire rating 2 hr | Fire strategy F-002 | Cert not provided | Insufficient info | Request test cert |
| 3 | Water absorption ≤ X% | Sec …, cl. … | 12% | Does not comply | Propose alt / RFI |

Verdicts: **Complies / Does not comply / Insufficient information**. Never upgrade "insufficient information" to "complies" without evidence.

## Common requirement categories to check

**Materials / products**
- Performance properties (strength, density, water absorption, thermal, acoustic).
- Ratings (fire rating, fire propagation/spread of flame class, slip resistance).
- Referenced standards (BS/EN, ASTM, ISO, ACI) and compliance certificates.
- Dimensions, tolerances, finish, colour/pattern (against approved sample).
- "Or approved equal" conditions and required submittal/approval before use.
- Country-of-origin / manufacturer approval where the spec names acceptable makers.

**Shop drawings**
- Agreement with contract drawings and other trades (coordination/clash).
- Dimensions, levels, setting-out, and interfaces.
- Compliance with detailed spec requirements and applicable code clearances.

**Executed work**
- Tolerances (verticality, level, surface regularity, alignment).
- Workmanship acceptance criteria from the spec.
- Required tests passed (e.g. cube tests, pull-off/adhesion, pressure test) with records.
- Inspection sign-offs (WIRs) at defined hold points.

## Close-out
End every compliance check with: overall verdict, list of non-conformities, and the specific missing information or certificates needed to close. Offer to raise an RFI for any genuine design/spec ambiguity uncovered.
