# Construction AEC Toolkit

Skills for construction engineering and architecture work — tuned for building, fit-out, and general-contracting projects.

## Skills

- **method-statement** — Produce a construction method statement (sequence of works, resources, plant, HSE controls, quality criteria) for a work activity such as blockwork, screeding, gypsum partitions, waterproofing, tiling, or MEP first/second fix. Trigger: "write a method statement for…".

- **rfi-spec-compliance** — Draft Requests for Information to the consultant, and check drawings, materials, or executed work against the project specification and codes, reporting compliance in a clause-by-clause matrix. Trigger: "raise an RFI…", "check spec compliance…".

- **submittal** — Prepare material/shop-drawing submittals and transmittals for consultant approval, built around a clause-by-clause compliance statement. Trigger: "prepare a submittal…", "material approval request…".

## How it works

Each skill gathers the essentials (activity, references, spec, standards), builds the document with a professional structure, and produces a Word deliverable (with PDF/Excel options) that leaves clearly marked placeholders for project numbers, dates, and names.

The skills lean on the built-in `docx`, `pdf`, and `xlsx` skills for polished output.

## Notes

These skills assist with drafting and organisation. They do not replace professional engineering judgment, a competent HSE risk assessment, or a qualified reviewer's approval. Verify all codes, standards, and spec references against the actual governing project documents for your region.
